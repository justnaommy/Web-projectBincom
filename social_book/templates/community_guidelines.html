Community Guidelines

Welcome to our community! These guidelines ensure a respectful, safe, and inclusive space for all users. By signing up, you agree to the following:

1. Be Respectful

Do not harass, bully, or attack others. Treat everyone with kindness, even when opinions differ.

2. No Hate Speech

Content that promotes racism, sexism, or any form of discrimination is strictly prohibited.

3. Stay On Topic

Only post or comment on relevant and appropriate content that aligns with the purpose of this platform.

4. No Spam or Promotions

Avoid posting repetitive messages, advertisements, or unrelated links.

5. Protect Privacy

Do not share anyone's personal or sensitive information without their consent.

6. Report Misconduct

If you see something inappropriate, report it. Help keep the community safe and welcoming.

Violation of these guidelines may result in content removal or account suspension.
